Dr. Bernhard Haslhofer is a Senior Scientist in the field of Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at the Austrian Institute of Technology. Before that, he worked as a Marie Curie fellow at Cornell University and as a PostDoc at the University of Vienna, where he also did his doctorate in the field of computer science. His research is generally very multidisciplinary, with his contribution mainly in the use of data science methods to answer scientific questions. His range of methods includes network analysis, machine learning, information retrieval and text mining. At the moment he is mainly concerned with the development of analytical methods for a better understanding of cryptoasset systems and the use of data science methods in an industrial context.
