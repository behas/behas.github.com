Bernhard Haslhofer is a Senior Scientist at the Complexity Science Hub Vienna, where he leads the CryptoFinance research group. He also teaches as an external lecturer at the Technical University of Vienna.

His general research focuses on finding and applying quantitative data science methods for gaining new insights from large-scale, connected datasets.

Before, Bernhard was a Senior Scientist at the AIT - Austrian Institute of Technology, where he acted as Thematic Coordinator in the Data Science & Artificial Intelligence research group.
