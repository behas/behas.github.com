Bernhard Haslhofer Research Profile

I am working as a senior scientist at the Complexity Science Hub Vienna, where I lead the CryptoFinance research group. I am also the co-founder of Iknaio Cryptoasset Analytics GmbH.

Research Interests

My general research interest lies in finding and applying quantitative methods to gain new insights from large-scale, connected datasets.

Currently, I am mostly working on cryptoasset analytics. Using quantitative methods, we answer a wide variety of research questions related to cryptoassets. I also lead the development of the GraphSense Cryptoasset Analytics Platform, and teach a course on Cryptoasset Analytics at TU Vienna.
