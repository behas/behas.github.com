Bernhard Haslhofer Research Profile

I am working as a senior scientist at the Complexity Science Hub Vienna, where I lead the Cryptofinance research group. I am also a co-founder of Iknaio Cryptoasset Analytics GmbH and act as court-certified expert for IT and cryptoasset forensics in particular.

Research Interests

My general research interest lies in finding and applying quantitative methods to gain new insights from massive network datasets.

Currently, I am mostly working on cryptoasset analytics. Using quantitative methods, we answer various research questions related to cryptoasset ecosystems like Bitcoin or Ethereum. I also lead the development of the GraphSense Cryptoasset Analytics Platform, and teach a course on Cryptoasset Analytics at TU Vienna.
