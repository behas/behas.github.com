Short bio: EN | DE

Bernhard Haslhofer, Cryptoasset Analytics & Blockchain Forensics Researcher

I lead the Digital Currency Ecosystems research group at the Complexity Science Hub in Vienna. My research develops computational methods for cryptoasset analytics and decentralized finance, digital crime and forensics, and large-scale disinformation.

I co-founded Iknaio Cryptoasset Analytics and serve as a court-certified expert in IT and cryptoasset forensics. I also teach courses on these topics at TU Wien.

Impact

Societal Impact: Data-driven methods that translate into safer digital ecosystems. A recent example is the takedown of over 370,000 illegal dark web sites, in collaboration with the Bavarian Central Office for the Prosecution of Cybercrime.
Industry & Adoption: The Iknaio spin-off and the open-source GraphSense platform, used by financial institutions, regulators, and investigators across sectors.
Policy & Regulation: Evidence to inform central banks (BIS), international forums (INTERPOL), and European policy debate (Forum Alpbach).
Standards & Open Infrastructure: The Europeana Linked Open Data pilot, ResourceSync (NISO), Open Annotation (W3C), and ongoing work toward harmonised standards for blockchain intelligence.
