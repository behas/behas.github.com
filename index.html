Short bio: EN | DE

I lead the Digital Currency Ecosystems research group at the Complexity Science Hub. My work focuses on developing computational methods to analyze cryptoasset ecosystems, combat cybercrime, and understand the socio-economic implications of decentralized finance. I co-founded Iknaio Cryptoasset Analytics and am a court-certified expert specializing in IT and cryptoasset forensics.

Research Interests

Digital Assets & DeFi: Developing computational methods to analyze technical and socio-economic aspects of cryptoasset and decentralized finance ecosystems.
Digital Crime & Forensics: Creating computational methods to understand and combat emerging cybercrime phenomena.
Disinformation: Analyzing structure and dynamics of large-scale disinformation campaigns.
