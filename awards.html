Awards & Recognition

Recognition for contributions to cryptoasset analytics, digital currency research, and open science initiatives.

Austria's premier award for innovative IT solutions. Recognized for groundbreaking research in cryptoasset analytics and blockchain forensics.

European research award recognizing outstanding contributions to monetary and financial research, awarded for work on decentralized finance compositions.

Awarded by the iSchools organization for excellence in information science research and scholarship.

Open Humanities Award (2013)

Recognition from the Open Knowledge Foundation for advancing open science and digital humanities research.

Marie Curie IOF Fellowship (2011)

Prestigious European Union fellowship supporting international research mobility and career development in academia.
